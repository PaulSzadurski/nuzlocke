PokeAPI Nuzlocke Run Tracker

App Information

Welcome to my Nuzlocke app made using jQuery and PokeAPI! Use this to keep track of your available and fallen Pokemon in your nuzlocke playthroughs. Enter the Pokemon (or Pokedex ID) of your choice in the first form, and add a nickname to the second form, then submit to add a Pokemon to your roster. To choose a Pokemon from the National Pokedex, check out Serebii's Pokedex here. To represent your losses and provide ever-present pressure during your nuzlocke run, click on a Pokemon once it is defeated to add it to the fallen section, and click again to bring it back to your roster if you made a mistake or you just can't bear the loss.

Nuzlocke Rules

Created by Nick Franco in his webcomic series, the Nuzlocke challenge adds a new level of challenge for skilled Pokemon trainers. In a Nuzlocke, you can only capture the first Pokemon encountered in a route, and any Pokemon that faints cannot be used for the rest of the run. Additionally, nicknames are strongly encouraged for the sake of bonding with your Pokemon, which is why I added it as a feature to this app!
